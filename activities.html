Activities

What we do

  • Free Yoga Classes and Shibirs: BYDK offers free daily yoga sessions from 4:30 am to 6:00 am at various locations in Pune, led by 18 volunteer trainers. Participants must complete a seven-day Shibir, held thrice a year, to join regular classes.
  • Scientific and Holistic Yoga Practice: Yoga is practiced scientifically at BYDK, focusing on mental, physical, and spiritual well-being during Bramha Muhurta (4:30 am - 6:00 am).
  • Community Outreach: BYDK supports Varkari and orphanage schools in the Sahyadri Mountains by donating food and supplies, funded through voluntary contributions.
  • Pandharpur Wari Service: BYDK organizes camps during the Pandharpur Wari pilgrimage, offering leg massages, health checkups, and free medicines for Varkaris.
  • Health Awareness: Regular workshops are held to raise awareness on heart disease, cancer, diabetes, and other health issues.
  • Weight Loss and Detox Camps: BYDK periodically hosts weight loss and detox camps on a no-profit, no-loss basis to promote health.
  • Training and Celebrations: BYDK has trained over 30,000 individuals in 26 Yoga Shibirs and celebrates occasions like Independence Day, Republic Day, Guru Pournima, and International Yoga Day.
